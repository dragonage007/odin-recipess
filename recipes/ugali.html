Ugali is a traditional staple food in many parts of Africa, made from maize flour and water. It's a simple yet filling dish that pairs well with vegetables, meat, or fish.

ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 4 cups of water

steps

  1. boil water in pot
  2. gradually add flour while stirring continuously.
  3. keep stirring until the mxture thickens and pull away from the sides.
  4. serve hot with vegetables or stew
